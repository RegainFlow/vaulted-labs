# VaultedLabs Privacy Policy

_Last updated: February 21, 2026_

VaultedLabs ("VaultedLabs," "we," "our," or "us") explains in this Privacy Policy how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use our website and services.

## 1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the VaultedLabs website and related online services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect:

- **Contact data:** email address you submit for the waitlist.
- **Usage and analytics data:** page views, clicks, navigation paths, and in-app interactions.
- **Device and technical data:** browser type, operating system, approximate location inferred from IP, and session metadata.
- **Security and anti-abuse data:** signals used to prevent spam and abuse, including CAPTCHA/Turnstile verification results.
- **Replay and heatmap data:** user interaction playback and aggregate click/scroll behavior.

## 3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and local storage to operate analytics and product telemetry, including session continuity and feature behavior.

We currently collect analytics by default and do not yet provide a cookie consent banner in-product.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use information to:

- provide and improve the VaultedLabs experience,
- analyze engagement and product performance,
- monitor reliability, troubleshoot issues, and secure the service,
- detect and prevent spam, fraud, and abuse,
- communicate service updates and waitlist-related messages.

## 5. Analytics, Replay, and Heatmaps

We use PostHog for product analytics, session replay, and heatmaps. This may include event tracking, page metadata, interaction signals, and replayed session behavior configured with input masking controls.

## 6. How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that process data on our behalf, such as:

- **PostHog** (analytics, session replay, heatmaps),
- **Supabase** (application backend/data services),
- **Cloudflare Turnstile** (bot and abuse prevention).

We may also disclose information when required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and platform integrity.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including security, analytics, legal, and operational needs.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No system is fully secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Eligibility and Children

VaultedLabs is intended for users age **18 and older**. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 10. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

## 11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the "Last updated" date when changes are posted.

## 12. Contact

For privacy requests or questions, contact: **support@vaulted-labs.com**
